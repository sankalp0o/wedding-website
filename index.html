Travelling to Udaipur

Connectivity

Udaipur has direct flights from Mumbai, Bengaluru, Delhi, Jaipur, Chennai, Kolkata & Hyderabad

It is also connected via Railway to

Availibility of tickets

December being the peak tourist season, flight fares might get expensive close to the travelling date so you should book early.

Things to do

If you are coming to Udaipur for the first time, we suggest you keep a day or two extra for seeing the city.

Even if you are not keen on visiting the heritage palaces or shopping for local handicrafts, being in the old city by the lake in the evening can itself be very rewarding.

Climate

December brings winters to Udaipur and the days tend to be pleasantly warm and nights gets slightly chilly. It would be advisable to carry a jacket.
