Travelling to Udaipur

Connectivity

Udaipur is reachable via direct flights from Mumbai, Bengaluru, Delhi, Jaipur, Chennai, Kolkata, Hyderabad, Ahmedabad. Udaipur is also well-connected with railways via trains reaching from Ahmedabad, Mumbai, Jaipur, Chittor and other places in Rajasthan, Delhi, Mysore etc.

Availibility of tickets

December is the peak tourist season for Udaipur. Flight fares would become more and more expensive as the travelling date closes on, so you should book tickets early.

Climate

December brings winters to Udaipur, and the days turn pleasantly warm while nights can be chilly. It would be advisable to carry a jacket.
